A classic guitar with a timeless design by Leo Fender.
Available in a wide range of price points, ranging from value for beginners to top performance for the pros.
Available in a wide range of finishes, such as sunburst, a wide range of colours, and various types of wood, such as Mahogany.
Available in HSS and SSS pickup configurations, with pickups ranging from vintage to standard to tex-mex.
The Stratocaster is an ergonomic guitar, with a 25.5" scale length, with necks made of woods such as basswood, maple, and alder.
